Lower School



Tr.Christine

Senior Teacher - Lower School

Lower primary comprises of class, one,two and three. Class one is located at the Funguo Estate ,Classes Two and Three are in Akila EstateEach class has four streams with an average of 30 pupils per stream.We have a team of professional and able teachers.

We strive to bring up an all round child. Apart from high acaedmic standards ,we excell in co-curricula activities also.such activities include Martial Arts,SKating,Brass Band,Spelling Bee and various clubs. We also parcipate in Music festivals.

Reading culture is cultivated in each child and our Library is well equippped with story books wriiten in both English and Kiswahili

Lower primary at Jonathan Gloag Academy is the best place for a child to start his/her primary education
