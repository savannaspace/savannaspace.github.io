Non-teaching Staff



The non-teaching staff play a very important note in enabling our school run smoothly. We set achievable goals on a firmly basis and aspire to reach them during the entire period. To do this, we use the Smart System

S - Specific goal is distinct and defines as much of the goals as possible and contains no ambiguos language
M - Measurement gives feedback and lets one to know when the goal is complete
A - Goals must be achieveable to individual or pupils
R - Feasible Realistic goals are challenging yet attainable within the given time frame
T - When ? Time frames must be aggressive yet realistic
